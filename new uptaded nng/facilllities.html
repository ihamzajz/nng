World-Class Facilities

Where comfort meets activity and wellness — supporting an active lifestyle for all ages with premium amenities.

Health & Fitness

State-of-the-art gymnasium with premium equipment, certified trainers, and wellness programs tailored to all fitness levels.

Sports Facilities

International standard squash and badminton courts, cricket nets, tennis, and football fields for competitive and recreational play.

Swimming Pool

Olympic-sized temperature-controlled pool with professional trainers, separate timing for families and advanced swimmers.

Elegant banquet halls and outdoor venues for weddings, corporate gatherings, and cultural events throughout the year.

Curated dining experiences featuring local and international cuisine, with both indoor and al fresco seating options.

Indoor Games

Table tennis, billiards, chess, and card rooms — a refined space for recreation and friendly competition.

Expansive, meticulously maintained green spaces ideal for family gatherings, morning walks, and outdoor events.

Sophisticated lounge area with premium seating, complimentary Wi-Fi, and a refined atmosphere for networking and relaxation.

Safe, modern play area designed for young members, featuring age-appropriate equipment under supervision.
